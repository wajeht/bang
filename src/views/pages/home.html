❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🤔 What is Bang?

Bang is a supercharged search shortcut system inspired by DuckDuckGo's !bangs. It lets you search across multiple platforms instantly using simple commands.

🎯 How do I use Bang?

Just type '!' followed by your preferred shortcut (e.g., 'yt' for YouTube) and your search query. Example: !yt cute cats

💫 What makes Bang special?

Unlike traditional !bangs, Bang offers customizable shortcuts, search history, and faster response times across all your favorite platforms!

🔐 Do I need an account?

While Bang works without an account, signing up lets you customize shortcuts and sync them across devices.

🌐 Which sites are supported?

Bang supports major platforms like YouTube, Wikipedia, GitHub, and hundreds more. Plus, you can add custom search engines!

💻 Is Bang open source?

Yes! Check out our Github repository to contribute or self-host your own instance.
